# Head of Software Engineering

**Company:** Jex Recruitment | Connecting top talent with leading companies
**Location:** Dubai, United Arab Emirates

## Job Details

- **Seniority level:** Executive
- **Employment type:** Full-time
- **Job function:** Engineering and Information Technology
- **Industries:** Financial Services

Director of Engineering

Job Purpose:

We are partnering with a leading FS organization to appoint a **Director of Engineering** to lead and scale a critical engineering function.

This is a **senior leadership role** focused on shaping engineering strategy, driving modern development practices, and establishing strong governance to ensure consistent, high-quality delivery across multiple teams.

The role is ideal for candidates currently operating at Exec **Engineering level** within complex, enterprise environments.

Key Responsibilities:

- Define and execute the **engineering strategy** aligned with business objectives and enterprise architecture
- Lead and scale multiple engineering teams, driving a culture of **innovation, quality, and continuous improvement**
- Establish and enforce **engineering governance frameworks**, including standards, controls, and best practices
- Drive adoption of modern engineering practices such as **Agile, DevSecOps, CI/CD, and cloud-native development**
- Ensure delivery of **secure, scalable, and high-performing technology solutions**
- Collaborate with product, architecture, and business stakeholders to align priorities and deliver value
- Monitor and improve engineering performance through **KPIs (velocity, quality, incident management)**
- Build, mentor, and develop high-performing engineering teams

What We're Looking For:

Leadership & Strategic Thinking

- Proven experience leading **large-scale engineering organizations**
- Strong track record in defining and executing **engineering strategy at enterprise level**
- Experience working closely with **business and architecture stakeholders**

Governance & Delivery Excellence

- Demonstrated experience implementing **engineering standards, frameworks, and governance models**
- Strong knowledge of **DevSecOps, CI/CD, and secure engineering practices**
- Focus on **quality, performance, and continuous improvement at scale**

Technical Background

- Experience with **cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)**
- Strong understanding of **microservices, APIs, and modern architecture patterns**

People & Stakeholder Management

- Ability to **lead, mentor, and scale engineering teams**
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills across senior leadership
