**Job Description**

At Network International, every second counts. As a leader in the fast-paced world of financial services, we thrive on innovation, agility, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. We recognize industry trends, the dual nature of technological advancements, and the scarcity of top talent. This is why we prioritize investing in our people.  
  
Here, you’ll work alongside industry experts to solve complex problems and leverage cutting-edge technology to stay ahead of the competition.  
  
We offer a culture where your skills are valued, your growth is prioritized, and your achievements are recognized—because winning isn't just our goal; it's our standard.  
  
We are seeking a highly experienced and visionary Chief Information Officer (CIO) –Acquiring to lead the technology strategy, innovation, and execution for our card acquiring  
  
business. The CIO will be responsible for driving the digital transformation, ensuring scalability, security, and efficiency in our acquiring platforms, and fostering innovation in payment technologies. This role requires deep expertise in card acquiring, payment processing, and regulatory compliance, combined with strong leadership skills to guide a high-performing technology team.  
  
**Responsibilities**

**Role Accountabilities:**

-   Develop and execute the technology strategy for the card acquiring business, aligning with overall company objectives.
-   Drive innovation in acquiring solutions, payment technologies, and digital transformation.
-   Evaluate emerging trends in payments, fintech, and regulatory frameworks to ensure a competitive edge.  
      
    

**Platform Development & Management**

-   Oversee the development, enhancement, and maintenance of the acquiring platform , products and services to ensure high availability, security, and scalability.
-   Lead development and integrations with banks, payment networks (Visa, Mastercard, etc.), and third-party fintech partners.
-   Ensure robust fraud prevention, security, and compliance with industry standards (PCI DSS, PSD2, EMV, etc.)  
      
    

**Operational Excellence & Performance Optimization**

-   Drive platform reliability, system resilience, and high-performance transaction processing.
-   Implement DevOps, CI/CD, and cloud-based solutions to enhance efficiency.
-   Ensure optimal system uptime, incident response, and disaster recovery processes.  
      
    

**Leadership & Team Development**

-   Build and mentor a high-performing technology team, fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation.
-   Work closely with product, operations, and risk teams to develop customer-centric solutions.
-   Develop strong partnerships with vendors, fintechs, and regulatory bodies.  
      
    

**Regulatory Compliance & Risk Management**

-   Ensure adherence to local and international regulatory requirements.
-   Implement security best practices to mitigate cybersecurity risks.
-   Collaborate with compliance and legal teams to maintain full regulatory compliance.  
      
    

**Education**

 **QUALIFICATIONS**

-   Educated to Degree level from a recognized university in computer science or relevant subject in technology. Preferably holds a Masters of Business Administration or Masters of Science in Management.  
      
    

**Qualifications & Experience**

-   15+ years of experience in payment processing, acquiring technology, or financial services.
-   Strong understanding of card acquiring, merchant services, payment gateways, and transaction processing.
-   Expertise in PCI DSS, EMV, tokenization, and secure payment protocols.
-   Experience with cloud computing, API integrations, and microservices architecture.
-   Proven leadership experience in managing technology teams and large-scale platforms.
-   Strong stakeholder management and ability to drive cross-functional collaboration.
-   Experience working in multi-cultural environments managing global teams  
      
    

**Preferred Skills**

-   Knowledge of emerging payment technologies such as blockchain, AI-driven merchant lending, and biometric payments.
-   Experience with builder customer centric omni-channels portals and mobile applications to facilitate self service and digital onboarding
-   Experience in scaling high-transaction volume platforms.
-   Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
