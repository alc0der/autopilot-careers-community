This is a no-nonsense, high-impact leadership role with a fast-growing SaaS business expanding into the UAE.

They're building out their first engineering presence in Abu Dhabi — and need a strong operator to come in, take control, and drive delivery.

If you like clear expectations, zero politics, and real ownership, this is that kind of environment.

- Drive prioritisation and focus — cut through noise
- Partner directly with CEO, Product, and Tech leadership
- Build and scale the UAE engineering function from scratch
- Hold teams accountable — challenge, unblock, deliver
- Bring structure, clarity, and execution discipline

## What They Want

- A strong leader first — not just a manager
- Someone who gets things done and delivers on time
- Comfortable being hands-on when needed
- Experience leading global / multicultural teams
- Able to operate confidently with C-level stakeholders
- Commercially aware — understands the business impact
- Not afraid to call things out and push for better

## The Culture Fit

- No politics. No fluff.
- High ownership, high accountability
- Clear communication, fast decision-making
- People who stay on top of things and take responsibility

## Why It's Interesting

- Founding engineering hire in the UAE

- Real influence on how engineering is run globally
