### Job Description: Engineering Lead

## Build AI‑Native Infrastructure

  
## Who we are

  
Recurix is a small, fast‑moving team building AI‑native workflows that truly improve how people work. We ship internally first and commercialize only when the impact is proven.

## What you’ll do

- Design and build core backend systems (APIs, pipelines, orchestration, storage) from first principles.
    
- Own end‑to‑end full‑stack flows: Python/Node back‑end → React front‑end → automated deployment.
    
- Create AI‑integrated workflows that solve real problems: no “AI theater.”
    
- Partner directly with the founders on technical direction and architecture.
    
- Drive technical decisions across product and design, keeping the codebase simple and maintainable.
    
- Continuously debug, refactor, and evolve the platform without hand‑offs or silos.
    

## What you bring

- Proven experience scaling backend services in production (Python, Node, Go, etc.).
    
- Comfort working across the stack; you don’t need to love UI, but you won’t be blocked by it.
    
- Hands‑on experience with AI/ML pipelines, prompt engineering, or LLM integration.
    
- Strong cloud‑infra skills (AWS/GCP), CI/CD pipelines, containers.
    
- Systems‑thinking mindset: you design for long‑term simplicity, not short‑term hacks.
    
- Ability to make autonomous decisions with limited information.
    
- Clear, concise communication
    

## Why it matters

  
Every product we ship depends on the infrastructure you build. Tight, modular systems let the whole team move faster, ship higher‑quality features, and think more clearly. This isn’t a peripheral role; you _are_ the team’s engineering backbone.

  
## What we offer

- Fully remote. we work across time zones, but expect overlap for real‑time collaboration.
    
- Straight‑forward salary and benefits package (performance based benefits, generous PTO, competitive compensation).
    
- No equity gimmicks – just clear compensation.
    

_We look forward to hearing from engineers who thrive on building solid, scalable systems that power real AI‑driven products.  
_
