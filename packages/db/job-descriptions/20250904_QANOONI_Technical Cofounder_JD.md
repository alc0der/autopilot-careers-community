# Technical Cofounder - Expert in LLMs & AI/NLP (LegalTech Focus)
## About Us:
We are an AI-driven LegalTech startup transforming the legal industry by developing cutting-edge solutions that automate complex workflows. Our mission is to leverage the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) to streamline tasks such as document review, drafting legal documents, and building intelligent AI agents that can manage end-to-end legal workflows. We’re looking for an LLM expert who has built complex AI systems from the ground up and can help lead the development of scalable, NLP-driven solutions for legal automation.

## Role Overview:
As the Technical Cofounder, you will be at the forefront of designing and implementing advanced AI systems that power our platform. You will lead the development of LLMs to automate legal processes such as document review and drafting, ensuring these systems are scalable, efficient, and accurate. The ideal candidate is an expert in LLMs, has a strong foundation in Python, and is experienced in building complex AI systems for real-world applications. Initially, you will contribute as an individual contributor (IC), using your expert Python and LLM skills to build core features, and later transition into a leadership role as the company scales.

Key Responsibilities:
- LLM Expertise & AI Development: Utilize your expert knowledge of LLMs to design, build, and optimize AI systems that handle tasks such as document analysis, contract generation, and legal research. Your role will involve creating, fine-tuning, and deploying LLMs to meet the specific needs of the legal industry.
- AI System Design & Architecture: Lead the design of complex AI architectures, ensuring seamless integration of LLMs into our platform to automate and optimize legal workflows.
- Document Automation: Develop AI-driven solutions for automating document review and drafting by leveraging the capabilities of LLMs, significantly reducing the time and cost of manual legal work.
- End-to-End Legal Workflows: Build intelligent AI agents that can manage entire legal workflows, from research and drafting to review and compliance.
- Custom Evaluation Metrics for LLMs: Design and implement custom evaluation metrics to measure the accuracy, reliability, and legal effectiveness of LLM outputs. This will ensure that the AI’s work meets the highest standards required in the legal sector.
- Performance Optimization & Scalability: Optimize and scale LLMs to handle vast amounts of legal data, ensuring efficient processing and high-quality output.
- Collaboration & Innovation: Collaborate with cross-functional teams to align AI solutions with user needs and the real-world demands of legal professionals. Stay at the cutting edge of LLM advancements and integrate state-of-the-art models into the platform.
