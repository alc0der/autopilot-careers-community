# Software Engineering Manager/Director

**Company:** Robert Walters
**Location:** Abu Dhabi, Abu Dhabi Emirate, United Arab Emirates

## Job Details

- **Seniority level:** Mid-Senior level
- **Employment type:** Full-time
- **Job function:** Information Technology
- **Industries:** Information Services

Our client is a fast-growing **global SaaS provider** with an established engineering hub in Europe and an expanding international footprint.

As part of their global growth strategy, they are launching a new office in Abu Dhabi and building out their engineering presence in the region.

## The Opportunity

This is a **foundational leadership role**-you will be the **first engineering hire in the UAE**, responsible for establishing and scaling the local engineering presence while leading distributed teams globally.

You will work closely with senior leadership, including the CTO, to drive delivery, build high-performing teams, and ensure alignment across international engineering functions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Lead and manage distributed engineering teams across Abu Dhabi and Europe
- Build and scale the UAE engineering function from the ground up
- Drive delivery, quality, and execution across backend and frontend teams
- Partner closely with Product and Architecture to align priorities and outcomes
- Own global resource allocation and capacity planning
- Establish scalable development processes and best practices
- Lead hiring, onboarding, and ongoing development of engineering talent
- Foster a strong engineering culture built on accountability and collaboration
- Use data and delivery metrics to guide decision-making and prioritisation

## What We're Looking For

- Proven experience as an Engineering Manager or in a similar leadership role
- Strong track record managing **distributed or global engineering teams**
- Experience leading multiple teams (backend and frontend)
- Ability to operate in **fast-growing, evolving environments**
- Strong stakeholder management and communication skills
- Experience in **resource planning, prioritisation, and delivery management**
- Background in software engineering (highly preferred)
- Experience hiring and developing engineering talent
- Data-driven mindset with a focus on measurable outcomes

## Nice to Have

- Experience scaling engineering teams from early stages
- Experience working closely with Product and Design
- Familiarity with AI tools in engineering workflows

## Why Join?

- **Founding role in the UAE** with high visibility and impact
- Opportunity to shape and scale a global engineering function
- Work directly with senior leadership, including the CTO
- International, collaborative environment
- Strong growth and career progression potential
- Flexible working arrangements
