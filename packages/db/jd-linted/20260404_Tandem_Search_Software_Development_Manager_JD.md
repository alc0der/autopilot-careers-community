# Software Development Manager (AI / Cloud)

Company: Tandem Search (on behalf of a fast-growing AI startup)
Location: United Arab Emirates (Fully Remote)
Employment type: Full-time

Our client, a fast-growing AI startup, is hiring a Software Development Manager (AI / Cloud) to lead a global engineering team and drive the delivery of cutting-edge, AI-powered solutions.

This is a fully remote, high-impact role working closely with senior leadership to shape the technical direction of the business.

## What you'll own

- Leading and mentoring a distributed engineering team
- End-to-end delivery of scalable, high-performance systems
- Driving engineering standards, architecture, and best practices
- Working across cloud infrastructure and AI-driven products

## Must-haves

- 7+ years in software engineering, including 3+ years in leadership
- Strong hands-on experience with C#/.NET and Python
- Solid background in cloud platforms (Azure or AWS)
- Experience working in startup or fast-paced environments
- Exposure to AI / machine learning (LLMs is a strong plus)
- Arabic-speaking is required
